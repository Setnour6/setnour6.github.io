[FaQ] Frequently Asked Questions

WARNING: The page content is set to be rewritten in the future

How do I join the server?

See the guide written on the discord server. Basically, in the Garry's Mod menu, Click "Find Multiplayer Game" -> Pill Pack Hide and Seek -> Gent's Hide-Run & Seek or Light's Hide and Seek.

How do you climb walls?

Right-click while touching and facing the wall to climb it. This only applies to seekers.

What does F1-F12 do?

Press f1 to toggle third-person camera on and off Press f2 to access the shop Press f3 to access your profile information with season and leaderboard information. Press f5 to take a screenshot Press f7 to show the emote wheel

Why does the server shut down?

This is so new updates can be added to the server, or there is a problem that needs to be resolved.

Who can I ask for questions about the server?

Feel free to ask any of our lovely staff members, they are always open to help. The community is also very helpful as our active player base is very welcoming to new players.
