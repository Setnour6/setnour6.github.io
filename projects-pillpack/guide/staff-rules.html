Hide & Seek Staff Rules

Failure to follow any of these rules may result in your immediate removal from the Hide & Seek Staff Team. 

  • Staff members are expected to be professional, serious, informative, and understanding when dealing with reports and while representing the server within all community platforms.

  • Staff members may not disrespect, harass, or grief any member of the Pill Pack H&S Community for any reason.

  • You may only use administrative commands while dealing with a situation.

    • (This includes teleporting, physgunning players, and checking logs.)

  • You may not request a promotion, nor request your report count throughout the month.

  • You may not ban, warn, or jail players without first gathering sufficient proof (screenshots, recordings) to justify the punishment(s).

  • Failure to respond to, or justify a punishment that is appealed/reported on the forums may lead to punishment.

  • You may not handle reports directly involving yourself or a member of your party unless there are no other active staff online.

    • You should attempt to call for additional staff via the #request-cmds channel in the Hide & Seek Staff Discord server, or via the admin @ chat.)

  • When applying for staff, in the field: "Did you read the staff rules:" the response should be your in-game name, IE: "Did you read the staff rules: gentoi".

  • Do not teleport higher ups without their permission, as it may result in a disciplinary action.

  • Do not spam teleport or any other commands on other staff or players.

  • You must always use SteamIDs when utilizing any staff commands.

    • (Targeting usernames has a chance to pull other people besides your target.)

  • You may not AFK in noclip

  • Don't aimlessly sit/pass through players hiding during rounds

    Only Lead Admins & Managers may deal with player vs staff reports.

  • Only Manager+ may deal with staff vs staff reports.

Trial Mod - Moderator may not noclip without an active report.

Senior Moderator - Admin may noclip with an active report.
Senior Admin - Manager may noclip at any time.
