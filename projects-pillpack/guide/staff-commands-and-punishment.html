[Hide & Seek] Staff Command and Punishment Guidelines

Pill Pack Hide & Seek Command & Punishment Guidelines

These guidelines are meant to be a general guide for staff. The punishment times listed are not required to be followed.

  • Ghosting (Includes Ghost-Snitching and Specific Snitching)

    • Warning

    • Timeout/Mute/Gag, 10 minutes (2 hours on repeated offences)

    • Blinding and/or Slaying/In-game Death (on repeated offences)

    • Ban, 2 days (on repeated offences)

  • Spamming

    • Warning

    • Timeout/Mute, 6 hours.

    • Ban, 1 day or permanent. (on repeated offences)

  • Teaming

    • Warning

    • Slaying/In-game Death

    • Kick

  • Begging for currency, to be a staff member or for giveaways

    • Decline

    • Warning

    • Timeout/Mute, 30 minutes (longer on repeated offences)

    • Kick

  • Racism and Discrimination (includes media and jokes, use of racial slurs)

    • Warning

    • Timeout/Mute/Gag, 6 hours (1 day for use of hard-r, hard-k, etc.)

    • Kick

    • Ban, 1 week (2 weeks for excessive discrimination) (on repeated offences)
      Content that contains jokes and such about racist groups (I.e. the KKK, etc.) are also subject to punishment.

  • Use or bypass of blocked words (includes media, use of non-racial slurs)

    • Warning

    • Timeout/Mute/Gag, 1 hour (6 hours on repeated offences)

    • Kick

    • Ban, 1 day (longer on repeated offences)
      Exemption: the r-word, since Gentoi uses it sometimes and doesn't mind too much about others using it (this may can change in the future)

  • Bullying, Constant Aggravation/Disrespect or Sexualizing Players

    • Warning (if low severity)

    • Timeout/Mute/Gag, 30 minutes. (2 hours on repeated offences)

    • Kick

    • Ban, 1 day (on repeated offences)

  • Bypassing Blocked Words or Usage of Blocked Words in Media

    • Warning (if low severity)

    • Deletion

    • Timeout/Mute/Gag, 10 minutes. (1 hour on repeated offences)

    • Kick

    • Ban, 1 day (on repeated offences)

  • Disrupting Gameplay (Server-wide, map-wide; includes hiders killing other hiders)

    • Warning

    • Slaying/In-game Death

    • Kick

    • Ban, 3 days (on repeated offences)

  • NSFW Content (Even as Memes, including NSFW Jokes)

    • Warning (if content is within a topic/discussion context in the chat)

    • Timeout/Mute, 24 hours

    • Kick

    • Ban, 1 week (on severe/repeated offences)
      Content which shows human genitals is an instant post removal and timeout. Content which shows illegal content (child 'ography, etc.) is subject to a permanent ban with no chance of appeal. Content that contains jokes about known paedophiles, as well as paedo "parties" (I.e. Diddy Party) can and well also be subject to punishment.

  • Doxxing (Even as a joke)

    • Ban, permanent

  • Impersonation

    • Timeout/Mute/Gag, until impersonation ends

    • Kick (on abuse or repeated offences)

    • Ban, until impersonation ends (on abuse or repeated offences)

  • Wrongful Command Usage

    • Warning

    • Demotion

    • Timeout/Mute, 6 hours

    • Loss of Points (TBD)

    • Loss of ELO (TBD)

    • Ban, 1 day (on high severity or repeated offences)

  • Abusing Bugs/Glitches and going on no-texture/out-of-bound zones

    • Warning (low severity)

    • Slaying/In-game Death (on repeated offences)

    • Loss of ELO (-2000 ELO)

    • Ban, 1 week or 1 month (high severity or repeating offences)

  • Using Seeker-Unreachable Spots

    • Warning

    • TP/Physgun Moving of offending player (if not listening or repeated offences)

    • Loss of ELO (if not listening or repeated offences)

    • Loss of Points (if not listening or repeated offences)

    • Ban, 1 week
      Note: spots that take a seeker over 2 minutes to climb without physical/teleportation assistance, starting from the ground level or a ground origin point, is considered unreachable by seekers

  • Useless/Unworthy/Unrequired Pings and Messages to Admins, Owners & YouTubers

    • Warning

    • Timeout/Mute, 2 hours (on repeated offences)

    • Ban, 1 day (on repeated offences)

  • Controversial/Political Topics

    • Warning

    • Timeout/Mute/Gag, depends on topic (more time on repeated offences)

    • Ban, depends on topic (on repeated offences)

  • Cheats/Hacks

    • Warning

    • Blind or Slay/In-game Death

    • Ban, permanent

  • Anything Not Listed

    • Up to staff's discretion

    • This post will be updated when needed

WARNING: The page content is set to be rewritten in the future
